09 / PRINCIPLES

What we are and what we are not

We are
  • Institutional. This is a government system handling official records. Every pixel earns its place.
  • Dense on operational screens. Staff process forty documents a shift. Density is respect for their time.
  • Generous on depositor screens. Occasional users need clarity, not density. One action at a time.
  • Restrained in motion. 150ms transitions. No bouncing. One pulse for the SSE stream and nothing else.
  • Honest about confidence. The AI tells the reviewer when it is uncertain. Human corrections leave visible traces.
  • Warm in neutrals. Paper, not clinic. Ink, not digital grey.
We are not
  • Playful. This is not Notion or Linear. No emoji reactions. No celebratory confetti.
  • Purple-gradient SaaS. No hero sections. No marketing aesthetic bleeding into the app.
  • Generic. No Inter, no Roboto, no system fonts. IBM Plex is the only family.
  • Loud in status. Saturated reds and greens assault the eye. Our semantics are muted.
  • Dark mode in v1. Half a dark mode is worse than none. We ship light first, right.
  • Chromatic everywhere. Color carries meaning. One teal, one terracotta, four semantic hues. That is all.
